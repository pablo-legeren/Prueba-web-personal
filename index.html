Football Match Prediction (Bachelor's Thesis)

Developed a match-by-match forecasting model for my Bachelor's Thesis, graded with honors (9.2/10), to predict final league standings with 85% accuracy. The model was based on a multivariable Poisson distribution, incorporating home/away effects and opponent strength via team-specific attack and defense coefficients. Implemented in RStudio, it used statistical inference, Monte Carlo simulations, and mean relative error (~14%) analysis across 1000 iterations to generate robust final ranking probabilities. Currently migrating the model to scikit-learn for improved scalability and performance.
